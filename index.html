About Me

Every aspect of web development inspires me, from the inception of an idea seen through to its final product, to the community of web developers themselves who influence how we interact with our world. Throughout my time in the web developing world I have known all developers to be big dreamers and advocates for postive change.
My ability to draw and love of sewing led me to study fashion design at the Art Institute of Seattle. In art school, I honed my drawing skills and took a multimedia class where I was introduced to graphic design.
